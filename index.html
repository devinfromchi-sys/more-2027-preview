About MORE

Bold. Empowered. Sparkling for His Glory.

The MORE Women's Conference is a two-day experience designed to encourage and equip women in every season of life. Through powerful teaching, worship, and authentic community, we are reminded that we cannot sparkle like jewels when we are dulled by fear and anxiety. To sparkle is to be bold and empowered in the One who created us.

On that day the Lord their God will rescue his people, just as a shepherd rescues his sheep. They will sparkle in his land like jewels in a crown.

Zechariah 9:16 NLT
